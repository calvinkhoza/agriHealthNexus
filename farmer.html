Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new product?

Navigate to the "My Products" section and click on "Add New Product". Fill in all the required details and submit the form.

How are payments processed?

Payments are processed weekly every Friday. You'll receive your earnings minus any platform fees directly to your registered bank account.

How can I track my deliveries?

All active deliveries are shown in the "Delivery" section with real-time updates on their status.

What should I do if an order is late?

Contact our support team immediately through the chat system or email support@agrihealth.com.
